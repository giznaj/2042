# 2042
2042 - A retro-modernized version of 1942 created in scratch.

## Instructions 
* Left Arrow - Move left
* Right Arrow - Move right
* Space bar - Shoot
# 

### Notes and Credits
Inspired by the popular game 1942 and modernized for the future!  Dive left and right to avoid the enemy and shoot down as many as possible.  The more enemies you take down, the more points you win and the closer you come to saving the Earth.
